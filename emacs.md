#Cursor Motion

##Characters
- ```C-f``` move forward
- ```C-b``` move backward
- ```C-d``` delete forward
- ```DEL``` delete backward

##Words
- ```M-f``` move forward
- ```M-b``` move backward
- ```M-d``` delete forward
- ```M-DEL``` delete backward
